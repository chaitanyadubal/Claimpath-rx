Use Case 1
🔍 Drug Coverage Lookup
Search by drug name, brand name, or J-code. Every payer's coverage normalized into one view.
Why this matters: Without this, an analyst opens 10 different PDFs to answer one question. "Humira", "adalimumab", and "J0135" all return the same results here — in seconds.
Use Case 2
📋 Prior Authorization Criteria
Full, structured PA requirements — diagnoses, step drugs, clinical scores, prescriber requirements, renewal terms.
Why this matters: PA criteria are the #1 barrier to drug access. Know exactly what each payer requires — without reading a 40-page PDF.
Use Case 3
⚖️ Cross-Payer Comparison
Same drug, multiple payers — differences auto-highlighted in red.
Why this matters: This is the core client deliverable — exactly where a drug is advantaged or disadvantaged across key payer targets.
Hold Ctrl/Cmd to select multiple payers.
Use Case 4
📅 Policy Change Tracker
Clause-level diffs — know exactly what changed and whether it matters clinically.
Why this matters: Payers update policies quarterly. Each change is classified as 🔴 Clinical (affects patient access) or 🟢 Cosmetic (formatting only).
